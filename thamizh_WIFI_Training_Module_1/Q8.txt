IEEE: 
	1. Institue of Electrical and Electronice Engineers
	2. They create the networking protocols and standards
	3. They create a technical standard that should be followed 
by all others more of like a guide template
	4. They mostly work on R&D part for creating new standards
	ex: they create standards like 802.11ac and 802.11ax
WFA
	1. Wi-Fi Alliance
	2. It is used to test the WiFi devices for consumer adoption
	3. Its main job is to test the devices and certify them
	4. A device can be marked as Wi-Fi only after they certify them
	ex: they should certify a device as compatible with Wifi 6 or Wifi 5
