To configure the static ip address for a pc , open its desktop and then 
choose IP configuration

Then enter the correct IP along with its correct subnet

To check the IP address you can move to cmd then enter ipconfig

And coming to modify the mac address , move to config menu and click
FastEthernet0 and then enter the correct mac address

To check the MAC address move to cmd , then enter ipconfig /all

We can use ipconfig command to see the ip address of a system and can
ping to it
