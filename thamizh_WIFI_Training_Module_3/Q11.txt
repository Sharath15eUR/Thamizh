Types of PPDU:

802.11a/g   legacy OFDM

802.11b		DSSS

802.11n		High throughput

802.11ac	very high throughput

802.11ax	high efficiency

802.11be  	Extremely high throughput


1. Legacy PPDU:

	short training field
	long training field
	Signal field
	Data field

2. HT PPDU

	legacy signal field
	HT-STF
	HT-LTF
	HT-SIH
	Data field

	L-HTF
	L-SIG-A
	VHT-SIG
	VHT-LTF
	VHT-STF
	VHT-SIG-B
	Data

4. HE PPDU
	L-STF
	L-LTF
	L-SIG
	HE-SIG-A
	HE-STF
	HE-LTF
	HE-SIG-B
	Data
